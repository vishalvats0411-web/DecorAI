About The Project

DECORAI was born from a passion for blending technology with creative design. I wanted to create a tool that empowers users to visualize their interior design ideas effortlessly. This project utilizes cutting-edge AI, including the Segment Anything Model (SAM), to intelligently identify and recolor walls, making the process of redecorating simple and fun.
